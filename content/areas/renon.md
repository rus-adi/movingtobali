## Quick take

Renon is often described as a **more breathable** part of Denpasar—useful for families who want city convenience but don’t want to feel stuck in constant hustle.

## Who this area fits

- Families who want practical city access
- Parents who value parks and routine-friendly living

## Tradeoffs

- Not a “beach village” feel
- You may still drive to specific beaches/activities

## What to test
- Your errands loop (grocery, pharmacy, activities)
- Morning commute timing
- Evening noise on your street
