## Quick take

Seseh can feel **quieter** than the busiest parts of Canggu while still being close enough to access the wider area’s services. It’s often a good fit for families who want **more calm at home**.

## Who this area fits

- Families who want a calmer base near the Canggu region
- Parents who don’t mind a bit more driving for specific errands or activities

## Tradeoffs

- Depending on where you need to go daily, commute time can add up
- Coastal areas can bring humidity and weather considerations

## What to test
- Morning commute timing into the areas you’ll use most
- Your evening routine (noise, sleep, mosquitoes)
- Grocery route
