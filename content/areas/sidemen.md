## Quick take

Sidemen is often chosen for **quiet + nature + slower living**. It’s less about convenience and more about lifestyle. Great for some families — too remote for others.

## Who this area fits

- Families who want calm, green space, and a slower rhythm
- Parents who are comfortable planning errands and travel more intentionally

## Tradeoffs

- Fewer services right nearby (depending on where you stay)
- Transport planning matters a lot

Guide: [Family transport options](/guides/family-transport-drivers-scooters-cars)

## What to test during a stay
- How you’ll do groceries and essentials
- How the location feels at night (quiet vs isolated)
- Internet reliability if you work remotely

## Nearby areas to consider
If you want “more services,” consider a base like [Sanur](/areas/sanur) and do Sidemen as a shorter stay.
