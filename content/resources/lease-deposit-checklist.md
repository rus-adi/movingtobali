## Copy/paste questions

### People + authority
- Who is the owner / authorized signer?
- Can you show proof of authority to rent this property?

### Money
- Total rent amount and currency
- Payment schedule (monthly / quarterly / yearly)
- Deposit amount and what it covers
- How deposit return is calculated and when it’s returned

### What’s included
- Electricity included? If not, how is it billed?
- Water included?
- Internet included and what speed/provider?
- Pool/garden maintenance included?
- Cleaning included?

### Maintenance
- Who fixes what?
- Typical response time?
- What happens if internet/power is unreliable?

### Exit + disputes
- Early exit policy
- What happens if there’s a dispute?
- Who is the contact person?
