## The calm month-one checklist

### Week 1: Basics

- SIM/eSIM + stable data
- Transport plan
- Grocery routine
- A basic clinic plan

### Week 2: Routines

- Sleep + food rhythm
- One daily outside activity
- One parent “anchor community”

### Week 3–4: Small upgrades

- Adjust commute
- Improve food defaults
- Add one extracurricular
