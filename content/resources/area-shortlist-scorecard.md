## Scorecard

Rate each area 1–5.

- Pace / calm
- Commute tolerance
- Walkability
- Kid-friendly activities
- Parent community fit
- Noise (street-level, not “area-level”)

## Tip

Don’t over-optimize before you arrive. Pick 2–3, then test-stay.
