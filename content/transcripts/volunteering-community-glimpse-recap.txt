What you’re seeing:
- A short, real-world glimpse of community life around Empathy School—parents, children, and staff sharing an activity together.
- The “secret” behind many calm relocations: repetition. When you return to the same place each week, kids make friends naturally and parents stop feeling like outsiders.

How to use this as a parent decision tool:
1) Notice the pace.
   - Does the energy feel calm or chaotic?
   - Would your child enjoy this style of shared activity?

2) Notice the adult presence.
   - Are adults consistently nearby?
   - Do kids look supported (not just “free-range”)?

3) Translate the vibe into a practical plan.
   - Pick one “anchor” for your first month: school pick-up, a weekly camp/program, a class, or a regular market/cafe routine.
   - Commit to showing up 4–6 times before you judge whether community is “working.”
